FI Event and Meeting Space Request Form

The Friday Institute offers events and meeting facility space for Friday Institute and College of Education projects and programs. We also rent event and meeting space to other NC State projects and programs that relate to the mission of the Friday Institute, when space is available.

Our hours of operations are 7:30am-6:00pm, Monday-Friday; events and meetings can be scheduled between 8:00am and 5:30pm, Monday-Friday.

Please fill out this form with details and pertinent information about your event and submit the form preferably 48 hours in advance of your meeting. Last minute or "day of meeting" requests for rooms, meeting space, or IT resources are not guaranteed, and may not be available on short notice.

All fields are required to submit the form. After submitting, you will be contacted by our event staff to confirm or discuss your request.
